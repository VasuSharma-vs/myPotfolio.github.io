Game Overview

After Heaven is a next-generation zombie horde survival game that pushes the boundaries of real-time GPU simulation. Unlike traditional crowd systems, it leverages Vertex Animation Textures and advanced shader state machines to render and animate up to 20,000 zombies simultaneously while maintaining 80-90 FPS performance. The game features dynamic environmental interactions, emergent zombie AI behavior, and a haunting atmosphere designed to deliver a truly immersive post-apocalyptic experience.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Horde Intelligence: Zombies exhibit adaptive behavior — pursuing players, investigating noise, and engaging in territorial conflicts. Group dynamics emerge naturally from individual AI routines.
  • Dynamic Spawning: Real-time spawning system manages agent lifecycle, ensuring constant challenge while optimizing GPU resources.
  • Environmental Hazards: Fire, radiation zones, and structural collapse events create dynamic obstacles that both player and zombies must navigate.
  • Ragdoll Physics Cascade: When zombies enter ragdoll mode, they're pooled for efficient re-animation, preventing memory bloat during intense combat.
  • Weapon Impact System: Complex hit registration validates every shot against thousands of agents, with proper feedback and state transitions.
  • Atmospheric Survival: Limited resources, environmental damage, and the constant threat of overwhelming hordes create strategic, tense gameplay.
